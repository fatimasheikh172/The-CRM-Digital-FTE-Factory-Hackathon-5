# TechCorp Brand Voice

## Overall Tone
- Friendly but professional
- Clear and simple
- Empathetic and helpful

## Email Style
- Greeting: "Dear [Name],"
- Ending: "Best regards, TechCorp Support Team"
- Under 300 words
- Use bullet points for steps

## WhatsApp Style
- Casual and short
- Max 2 to 3 sentences
- No long greetings

## Web Form Style
- Semi-formal
- Medium length
- Numbered steps when needed
