About TechCorp FTE

Revolutionizing customer support with AI

Our Mission

To empower businesses with intelligent, always-available customer support that combines the speed of AI with the empathy of human understanding. We believe every customer deserves instant, accurate, and personalized assistance.

🎯

What We Do

TechCorp FTE is an AI-powered customer success platform that handles support tickets across Email, WhatsApp, and Web channels. Our system uses advanced sentiment analysis and intelligent routing to deliver exceptional experiences.

💡

How We're Different

Unlike traditional chatbots, our AI maintains context across channels, understands customer emotions, and knows when to escalate. With 84% resolution rate and 1.4s average response time, we set new standards.

Our Technology

Built on enterprise-grade infrastructure: FastAPI backend, PostgreSQL database, Kafka messaging, and Kubernetes orchestration. Powered by Gemini 1.5 Flash for lightning-fast AI responses.
